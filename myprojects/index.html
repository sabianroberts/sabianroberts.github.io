Dise

2016—2020

Now this is the project I contributed most of my time to. The Dise project was a massive experiment to see how much music I could make with just samples. My sample library was limited, but I had most of the packs from Ampify Launchpad on iOS, so I was able to make a 14 track album in 2017 called Launchpad. I created a couple of other albums, and in 2020, I released the final album, Sky Web EPG, for the Dise project and announced that the project was ending. Why? Simply because, it was getting bad and it wasn't very original to be honest. Anyone could just look at my albums, analyse the tracks and find all the original samples I used. Also, I wasn't enjoying making new music for the project and more like I had all my creativity taken out of me. I stopped using the Dise alias in July and now I release original music under my real name.

Lost and Dead

2017—2018; 2020

This is probably one of my favourite project and for a good reason. The story behind this Half-Life mod is that amateur developer Rafael Soares was looking on the Steam Forums for some help on a GoldSrc project and I hit him up, and the rest is history. We made Lost and Dead to many people's enjoyment and we still love it to this day despite it not being in development anymore. The project was revived as Lost and Dead Classic in 2020 but didn't last very long.

Half-Life: Zombies Ate My Neighbours

2018—Present

This Half-Life mod is the project I'm currently spending most of my time working on right now (other than this very website), and it also has a bit of history. Again, I made this mod with Rafael, but not only him, I'm making this with a big team of other people. This originally started out in August 2018 as a port of Zombies Ate My Neighbors over to the Half-Life Engine, but the project was ditched quickly. I revived it in February 2020, and changed it to a ZAMN-HL crossover (hence why the code name for the game is zamnhl). I'm planning on releasing this mod on November 21st, 2021.

Sky Web EPG

2020

This project I actually didn't start making myself. It was someone else named Gazlene in the Old Digibox Discord server. However, since it was on GitHub, I helped make nearly all of the Sky Web EPG. It is a pixel-for-pixel perfect re-creation of the Sky EPG from 2005—2010. Even music works too! It comes from an M3U audio livestream I made and the music set changes depending on the time of day.
